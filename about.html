Bio:

As an Accountant (ACCA), my expertise encompasses business management, accounting, and inventory management, complemented by prior experience in web design. Exploring web design as a personal interest, I often burrow into the intricacies of captivating websites that caught my attention.

Recognizing my proficiency, ASL Real Estate enlisted me to revamp their website. Over four years, I diligently redesigned and managed their expansive platform before passing it on to another individual.

Driven to enhance my skills, I pursued certification with CIW, earning recognition as a Certified Web Professional. My ambition now leads me to pursue an AAS in web development from Wake Tech Community College. With two successful real estate website projects under my belt, I'm poised to further expand my expertise in this field.

At Rodney Bradshaw Designs, specializing in facilitating small business owners in obtaining effective websites is my paramount objective. I firmly believe in crafting websites that promptly convey pertinent information to visitors. Employing user-friendly navigational menus, seamless designs optimized for various devices, and engaging animations, I strive to enhance user experience.

My approach revolves around fulfilling the specific desires of my clients for their websites. I advocate the use of wireframes to aid clients in visualizing the layout and expediting the launch process of their pages.
