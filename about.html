Bio:

As an Accountant (ACCA), I have the skills of business management, proper accounting and inventory management infused with previous web designing skills. I have been doing web designs as a hobby interest. When I visit a site that I found interesting, I researched how it was done.

This hobby of mine was recognize and I was asked by ASL Realestate to redesign their site. A realestate site is vast and requires lots of time. I redesign it and maintained it for over 4 years and passed it on to another.

Having decided to go further, I did certification with CIW, became a Certified Web Professional. I want to go further and soon to obtain AAS in web development from Wake Tech Community College. Already have designed and maintained two real estates web sites.

At Rodney Bradshaw Designs, helping the small business owners get a web site is what I do best. I belief in creating websites that makes the visitors get informed quickly about what they are looking for. With easy navigational menus, smooth designs, from mobile to desktop and attractive animation.

Designing what my clients want in their site. I also belief using wireframes, so as to help the client envision what their site will look like, and get their page launch faster.
